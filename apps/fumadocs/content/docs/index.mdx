VISP turns phones into remote cameras for the OBS production you already
built. Sign in with Twitch or Kick, import a ready-made scene collection, go
live from a phone or browser, and keep scenes, alerts, and graphics running at
home.

VISP never asks for your Twitch or Kick stream key. The hosted beta is free.
The dashboard starts in **Simple** mode with labeled copyable URLs; switch to
**Advanced** for RTMP fallbacks, read credentials, and latency tuning. If you
get stuck during setup or on the dashboard, ask **Seppo** for help.

## What VISP does not do

VISP does not transcode, bond networks, host OBS, or manage your destination
stream key. MediaMTX accepts one publisher per path: the first wins until it
disconnects.
